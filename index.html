How It Works
Simple, Fast, Accessible
01

Deaf User Signs

The deaf user performs a sign in front of the camera. MediaPipe tracks hand landmarks and translates the gesture into text or speech in real time.

02

Hearing User Speaks

The hearing user speaks naturally or types a message. The system converts speech to text via Google STT, then maps it to sign language visuals.

03

Instant Translation

Both users receive translated content immediately — no sign language knowledge required from either side.

How BridgeSign Helps Deaf Individuals

Medical Appointments

Deaf patients can communicate with doctors and nurses directly without needing a sign language interpreter present.

Education Access

Students with hearing impairments can participate in classroom discussions and communicate with teachers effectively.

Daily Interactions

From shopping to public services — deaf individuals can interact with hearing people independently in everyday situations.

Family Communication

Families with both deaf and hearing members can communicate naturally without barriers or misunderstandings.
